Features

Short, focused lessons on digital rights, online privacy, data protection, cybersecurity, internet governance, AI policy, and more.

Lesson Quizzes

How It Works

Your journey starts in three steps

From your first visit to the weekly challenge — here's how DIRI works.

01

Create your account

Sign up with your email or social account. It takes 30 seconds and gives you access to everything DIRI offers.

02

Learn at your pace

Study short learning cards on digital rights topics. Check your understanding with quick quizzes. Ask Mr. DIRI anything.

03

Compete and stay sharp

Take the weekly quiz challenge, climb the leaderboard, and earn recognition. New questions every week from current issues.
